Delete Your Account

To delete your account from Outstanding Success Catalysts, go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account in the app.

Alternatively, you can send an email to support@outstandingsuccesscatalysts.com.

Data Handling

  • ✅ Personal data deleted within 30 days.
  • ✅ Some anonymized data may be kept for legal compliance.
  • ✅ Once deleted, your account cannot be restored.
